My Project Proposal

1. Title and Topic: Title:

"Valorant: The Ultimate Guide": This website will focus on the popular tactical shooter game Valorant, covering gameplay mechanics, agent abilities, maps, strategies, and esports scene.

2. Why I Chose This Topic:

I chose this topic because Valorant is a game that I enjoy playing. It combines strategy, skill, and teamwork, making it an exciting project to do. My goal is to create a webpage that helps both new and experienced players improve their gameplay and give a good information and facts about the game in general.

3. Content Breakdown:

Home Page: Introduction to Valorant, including a brief history of the game and an overview of what users can find on the website.

Page 1 - Gameplay Basics: Overview of game mechanics, including basic movement, and shooting mechanics.

Page 2 - Agent Abilities & Roles: Breakdown of popular agents, their abilities, and their roles in the game.

Page 3 - Maps & Strategies: A detailed look at each map, including some best strategies, and site control tips.

Page 4 - Esports and Competitive Scene: Information about the Valorant esports scene, major tournaments, and top professional teams.

Page 5 - Agent & Weapon Stats Table: A table featuring weapons, including damage, fire rate, and utility effectiveness.

4. Form and Table Information:

Form Fields: The form will collect visitor feedback and include fields such as: Name, Email, Favorite Agent, Rank (Iron to Radiant), Comments/Suggestions.

Table Data: The table will include Valorant statistics, featuring data like: Agent Name, Role, Ability Cooldowns, Weapon Name, etc.

5. Collecting Information & References:

I will gather information from official game sources, community guides, and esports coverage sites. I will also include my own knowledge and experience in the game.

Reference 1: Official Valorant Website (www.playvalorant.com)
Reference 2: Liquipedia Valorant (liquipedia.net/valorant)

Additional references will include patch notes, YouTube gameplay guides, and esports tournament websites.
